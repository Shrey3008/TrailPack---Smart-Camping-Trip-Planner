How it works

Three steps to a perfect pack

From trip idea to confident departure in under five minutes — no spreadsheets, no missing gear.

01

Describe your trip

Pick terrain, season, dates, and group size. Add a location and we'll pull the forecast automatically.

Frequently asked questions

Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Subscriptions are month-to-month with no contract. Cancel from your profile and you'll keep premium features until the end of your billing period, then drop to Free with all your data preserved.
What happens to my trips if I downgrade?
Nothing is deleted. Trips beyond the Free limit (3 active) become read-only. Reactivate Pro any time to unlock them again. Trip history is always preserved.
Is there a student discount?
Yes, 50% off Pro with a verified .edu email. Email students@trailpack.app with your school ID and we'll set up a discounted billing plan.
Do you offer annual billing?
Yes — toggle the billing switch above the pricing cards. Annual saves 20% across Pro and Group tiers (effectively 2.4 months free per year).
What payment methods do you accept?
All major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover) plus Apple Pay and Google Pay via Stripe. We never see or store your card details.
Is my trip data private?
Yes. Your trips are private by default and only visible to collaborators you explicitly invite. We never sell user data, and we don't show ads.
Can I use TrailPack offline?
Pro users can export a printable PDF checklist for offline use. A native mobile app with full offline support is on our 2026 roadmap.
Do you offer discounts for outdoor education programs or non-profits?
Yes. Scout troops, outdoor education programs, and registered 501(c)(3) non-profits get 30% off the Group tier and a custom onboarding session. Email partnerships@trailpack.app to apply.
